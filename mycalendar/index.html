MyCalendar

MyCalendar is a calendar app that syncs your Google Calendar events to your phone and to your car's built-in display (Android Automotive OS), so your schedule is visible on the road without needing to touch your phone.

What it does

  • Signs in with your Google account and reads your Google Calendar events (all calendars on the account: primary, work, shared, birthdays).
  • Displays your schedule in a day/agenda view, with a wide layout for car head-unit screens.
  • Lets you add a new event, which is written straight back to your primary Google Calendar — it then appears on your phone, the web, and every other device synced to that account.
  • Keeps a local, offline copy of your last sync so your schedule is still visible when your car has no signal.

Data use

MyCalendar only requests access to Google Calendar (read your events, and create events you add in the app) and your account email address (to show which account is signed in). It does not request access to any other Google data, and calendar data is not sent to or stored on any third-party server — it stays between your device and Google.

Sign-in

Because this app also runs on car head units that may not have Google Play Services installed, sign-in uses Google's device/QR code flow: the app shows a QR code, you scan it with your phone, and approve access there.
